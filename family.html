People I Love Most

This special section are meant to highlight these two people that i called Ayah and Ibu, who have made my life bearable. Ayah and ibu has made my life so much easier without me realizing it up until I have become an adult. They always work hard to give me the things that I need and want in life that it makes me realize that being a parents are not as easy as how they make it looked like. They never really show their struggles to satisfy the needs of their family. Always try and try until they know that we got everything that we need.

Ofcourse we did not always get what we want. Ibu and Ayah always told me that "if you want something, save your own money". I remember how I bought my first phone using my duit raya and school allowance. And ever since, I always worked on it myself most of the time. Even on things that they can obviously afford for me, they always encourage me to be more responsible with money and the way I plan and manage life for the best future. Growing older, I realize that this practice are not all about money. It thought me so much more as a person, how your own hardwork can bring you to success and to the things that you want. I also learn how responsibility are so important in life that living with it can make me closer to my target in life.

Thankyou for all your love and sacrifices that have been made while raising our family, Ayah and Ibu.
